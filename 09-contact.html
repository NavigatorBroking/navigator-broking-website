Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with Navigator Broking

How quickly can you assess my situation?

I can provide an initial assessment within 24 hours of receiving your details. For more complex cases, I may need 2-3 days to thoroughly analyze all options.

Do you charge fees for initial consultations?

No, initial consultations and assessments are complimentary. I only earn commission from successful loan settlements, so my interests are aligned with yours.

Can you help if I've been rejected by banks before?

Absolutely. Many of my clients come to me after bank rejections. With access to 60+ lenders and specialist loan products, I often find solutions where others can't.

Do you work with clients outside Melbourne?

Yes, I service clients across Australia. While I specialize in Melbourne's bayside suburbs, I help clients nationwide with phone and video consultations.
